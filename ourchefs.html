Our Chef's

Gordan Ramsay

Food & Beverage Director

Gordan graduated Summa Cum Laude from Johnson and Wales University in Norfolk, Virginia. Julia Child

Catering Manager

Julia arrived in Sushi House in June of 2001 after just completing his senior year at Penn State. Graduating with a degree in Management, Julia set a goal of moving to the mountains for a year...that was nine years ago. She chose Sushi House because it offered “the ability to work in city level restaurants without the city stop lights”.

Bobby Flay

Kitchen Manager

Bobby joined the Sushi House team August of 2008 as a Line Cook. Ahmad graduated from the University of Arkansas with a BS in Food, Human Nutrition and Hospitality. Prior to Sushi House, Bobby was a Diet Technician at Washington Regional Medical Center in Fayetteville, Arkansas. Bobby’s passion for food and an active life style brought him to Sushi House. After 4 years of cooking, he was excited to take over the position of Kitchen Manager of Sushi House. Bobby’s hobbies include snowboarding, golfing and camping with his dog Shiner. He prides himself in being good in every sport, just ask him.
